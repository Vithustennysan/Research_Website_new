The fifth meeting of the Data Engineering & Research (DEAR) Group was held last week, graced by the special presence of Prof. Mahesan Niranjan from the University of Southampton, UK. Thank you Pubudu Chandima & Hasitha Karunathilake for the compelling presentation on the Implementation of Automated Inspection & Defect Detection Mechanism for Porcelain Items. And my heartfelt gratitude to Damayanthi Herath madam, Dharshana Athauda sir, and Prof. Mahesn Niranjan sir for sharing their invaluable insights. It was a privilege to meet and engage with such knowledgeable minds.
